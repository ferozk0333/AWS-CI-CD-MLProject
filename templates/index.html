Student Exam Performance Indicator

Aim of the Project

The aim of this project is to predict the performance of students in their mathematics exams based on various factors such as gender, race, parental level of education, lunch type, test preparation course, and scores in reading and writing.

Problem Statement

This project addresses the issue of understanding and predicting student performance in exams. By analyzing different factors that contribute to academic success, educators and parents can provide better support and resources to students who need it the most.

Solution

Using a machine learning model, this application takes in various inputs related to a student's background and previous scores to predict their likely performance in mathematics exams. This helps in identifying students who may need additional help and resources.
